Child Safety Standards

Last updated: May 2026  |  Simplex Labs  |  mostvaluedplanter@gmail.com

Simple-x is a dating application strictly for adults aged 18 and older. We are committed to the safety of children and maintaining a platform that has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form.

Age Requirement

Simple-x requires all users to be 18 years of age or older to create an account. Users must confirm their age during account creation. Accounts found to belong to minors are immediately terminated.

Zero Tolerance for CSAE

Simple-x has a strict zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any content that exploits, endangers, or sexualizes minors. This includes but is not limited to images, videos, text, or solicitation of any kind involving minors.

Any user found to be sharing, soliciting, or distributing CSAM will be immediately banned and reported to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant law enforcement authorities.

Reporting Mechanisms

Simple-x provides in-app reporting tools that allow users to report any content or behavior that violates our child safety standards. Reports can be submitted directly through the app on any user profile or message. All reports are reviewed promptly.

To report child safety concerns outside the app, contact us directly at mostvaluedplanter@gmail.com.

Compliance with Law

Simple-x complies with all applicable child safety laws, including 18 U.S.C. § 2258A (reporting CSAM to NCMEC) and all relevant regional and national statutes. We will cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations involving child safety.

Content Moderation

Users can block and report other users at any time. Reported accounts are reviewed and actioned appropriately. We maintain the right to remove any content or terminate any account that violates these standards without notice.
